The Life of John Paul Jones

  • 1747: John Paul is born in glorious Scotland.
  • 1760: John Paul begins his naval career at the age of 13.
  • 1768: John Paul becomes a captain after safely returning a ship to port when both the captain and first mate died of yellow fever.
  • 1770: John Paul arrives in America and changes his name to John Paul Jones.
  • 1775: John Paul Jones volunteers his services to the Continental Navy.
  • 1776: John Paul Jones raises the first American ensigna over a ship, the Grand Union Flag.
  • 1778-1779: John Paul Jones wrecks the British during the American Revolution.
  • 1999: John Paul Jones is posthumously pardoned by the town of Whitehaven for raiding it during the Revolution.
